Frequently Asked Questions

What are auction lobbies?
Auction lobbies are Daily Auctions that offer BUB Tokens for BNB based on the BUB and BNB pool at the end of the day. When you enter with BNB, you will receive a propotioniate amount of BUB to stake. Available BUB Tokens tokens start at 2.5 million, down 4-5000~ per day to a MINIMUM of 100,000 tokens available.
Where does the BNB go?
70% of BUB that enters the daily Auction Lobby is pooled and distributed back out to Stakers.
What is staking?
You can stake your BUB token for a fixed number of days to earn interest on them and get BNB dividends from the daily auction pool. Max stake time is 365 days. Every fifth day will earn a bonus amount of interest on staked BUB.
At the end of every day a daily stake pool of BUB Tokens will be calculated and it will be shared and allocate to all the open stakes between all the open stakes based on their staked tokens amount which will be available for user to withdraw when the stake ends.
Do I get BNB dividends for staking?
Yes! The biggest benefit of Staking BUB is BNB dividends. At the end of each day a BNB dividends pool will be calculated and allocated to all the stakers, distributed evenly based on their stake amount. The BNB dividends pool comes from the daily auction lobby total entry amount.
How do I know if my stake has ended?
The button will say "collect" on your active stakes list, otherwise unfinished stakes will say "cancel".
What if I end my stake early?
You will still get ALL BNB dividends. However you will be penalized BUB Tokens.
How is dividends pool calculated?
70% of BNB that enters the daily Auction Lobby is pooled and distributed back out to Stakers. The remaining 30% is collected as an owner share.
When will I receive my BNB dividends?
When your stake ends, click "collect" and you will receive your BNB dividends. You may also cancel a stake, doing so will still give you all of your BNB dividends. However, returned BUB Tokens are penalized a % based on time left for early cancellations.
